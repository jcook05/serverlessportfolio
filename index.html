About Me

I'm an AWS Certified Software Engineer/Architect specializing in serverless, microservices, cloud engineering and containerization. I help customers migrate solutions into AWS and containerized or serverless microservice applications. Specializing in AWS my preference for serverless functions is Lambda however I use cloud agnostic tooling such as the Serverless Framework to allow for ease of future cloud migration. Serverless is the next evolution of software and presents many cost saving and rapid development opportunities. This portfolio is 100% serverless and 100% free on AWS Free Tier!
