About Me

I'm an AWS Certified Solutions Architect Professional specializing in serverless, microservices, containerization and cloud native solutions. I help customers migrate solutions into AWS and containerized or serverless microservice applications. Specializing in AWS cloud native, my serverless preference for functions is Lambda, however, I'm sensitive to the desire of clients to remain cloud agnostic. That being said, I leverage cloud agnostic tooling such as the Serverless Framework and Terraform when possible.


Serverless is the next evolution of software and presents organizations with many cost saving and rapid development opportunities. I have a high speed, low drag philosophy and am passionate about implementing high quality solutions that adhere to the latest cloud architecture best practices.


It is possible to create Single Page Apps and Static Serverless Websites 100% free in AWS. This site is a static site written in React.js with 100% CICD and is currently 100% free in AWS free tier. Microservices running as Lambda functions, when coupled with the correct architecture design, have the potential to save money both in infrastructure and operations cost, thus reducing the total cost of ownership.
